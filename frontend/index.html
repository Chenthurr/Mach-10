Key Capabilities

Intelligent Features for
Safer Cities

MACH-10 leverages cutting-edge AI to transform ordinary traffic infrastructure into an intelligent, responsive system that prioritizes human safety and environmental sustainability.

Emergency Green Waves

Automatically clear routes for ambulances and fire trucks, reducing life-threatening delays by up to 40%. AI detects emergency vehicles and creates a seamless green corridor.

Dynamic Pedestrian Crossings

Real-time crowd detection adapts signal timing to pedestrian movements, ensuring safety at busy crosswalks near schools, hospitals, and commercial areas.

Invisible Road User Recognition

Detects and prioritizes cyclists, rickshaws, and other non-motorized vehicles often overlooked by traditional systems, ensuring inclusive urban mobility.

Pollution-Aware Routing

Minimizes traffic impact in sensitive zones near schools and hospitals. Reduces idling times and optimizes flow to decrease emissions by up to 30%.
