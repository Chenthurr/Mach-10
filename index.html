Urban mobility is under pressure.

Congestion, pollution, and safety risks are now everyday realities in growing cities. The impact is felt most by emergency responders, pedestrians, cyclists, and non-motorized commuters.

  • 1 Life-threatening delays for ambulances and fire trucks due to static signal plans and congested corridors.
  • 2 Unsafe crossings for pedestrians at busy junctions with no real-time understanding of crowd movement.
  • 3 Invisible road users such as cyclists, rickshaws and micro-mobility vehicles ignored by legacy systems.
  • 4 Pollution hotspots near schools and hospitals caused by idling vehicles and poorly timed signals.

Where MACH-10 intervenes

By plugging into existing CCTV infrastructure and applying computer vision plus rule-based decisioning, MACH-10 transforms each junction into an intelligent node that understands who is on the road, what they need, and how signals should adapt.

The result is a shift from car-centric throughput to human-centric, safety-first operations.

What MACH-10: TrafficOps+ delivers

A plug-and-play intelligence layer that sits on top of existing signals to orchestrate traffic dynamically in response to real-time conditions.
